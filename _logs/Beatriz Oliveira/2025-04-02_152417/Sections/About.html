Beatriz Oliveira is a passionate web innovator with over 25 years of experience in Microsoft technologies. As the founder and CEO of BindTuning, she leads a team that crafts cutting-edge solutions for Microsoft 365.

Beatriz graduated top of her IT Engineering class, beginning her career at Ernst & Young. She co-founded CentralBiz, a successful Microsoft services company, and after selling her share, she post-graduated in Communication and Design at IADE. This well-rounded expertise fuels BindTuning's innovative approach.

In 2008, Beatriz overcame a serious accident and refocused her second company, Bind, on web themes development. This shift led to the creation of BindTuning in 2011.

A respected speaker at international events, Beatriz is a Microsoft Regional Director, MVP alumni, and former president of IAMCP Portugal. She actively champions STEM education, inspiring young minds.
